## Common Mistakes to Avoid (From Industry Knowledge)

### Deliverability
- ❌ Sending cold emails from main domain (nexus.lat)
- ❌ Skipping warmup or cutting it short (<21 days)
- ❌ Sending >40 emails/day per mailbox
- ❌ Not validating emails before sending
- ❌ Using image-heavy signatures
- ❌ Including tracking links in first email

### Copy
- ❌ Generic copy without personalization
- ❌ Emails longer than 100 words (Email 1) or 50 words (Email 2)
- ❌ Using spam trigger words (free, guarantee, limited time)
- ❌ Not ending with a question
- ❌ Badmouthing competitors directly
- ❌ Making claims without data

### Targeting
- ❌ Contacting staffing/recruiting agencies (our competitors)
- ❌ Not deduplicating across workflows (double-contacting same person)
- ❌ Contacting companies <10 or >500 employees
- ❌ Reaching out to roles we can't actually fill

### Process
- ❌ Not documenting A/B test results
- ❌ Running multiple test variables simultaneously
- ❌ Not updating exclusion lists after new clients sign
- ❌ Declaring A/B test winners too early (<100 sends per variant)
